Dashboard

Stats

The dashboard displays static information such as total case matters, open cases, pending cases and close cases,as well as overall info of law management.

Graphs

The dashboard also presents graphs for quick reference, including case matter status, total lawyers and judges.

Leads

Law management

The law management app seeks to provide a comprehensive system for efficient management of daily workflows and business operations in law firms.

Leads management

Manage client requests and leads seamlessly with an integrated CRM system.

Matters

Case matter details

You can manage the case matters related details and reason of case matters.

Client details

To manage the client details you have options client name, phone number, emails and client residential address etc.

Case trials management

To manage the case trials in case hearing and next hearing dates and assign to tasks.

Tasks management

it's simple to create new tasks and plane activities according to your needs, making it possible to handles a matters.

Lawyers management

Easy to make lawyers from partner. In the partner view, you have to just enable the option 'Is lawyer and define wages arrangements by lawyers.

Documents management

You can manage the case matter related required documents

Case evidences

You can keep track of every evidence with favor and full details & documents related matter.

Parties involved

You can keep the witness information, victim information and favor information.

Invoicing management

Invoice your clients in way that's easy from them end effective for you.

Reports

You can print a PDF report of any case/matter with details

Configurations

Case matter category
