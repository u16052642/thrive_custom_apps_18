Payment From Website

To pay with Payfast, select payfast as payment method and Click Pay Now button. You will be redirected to Payfast payment page.

You will redirect the payment page.

After selecting a payment method, proceed to pay using your chosen method.

Upon successful payment, you will be directed to the Thrive confirmation screen.

Payment From Sale Order

To pay with sale order, create sale order and click Preview button

Now click Sign & Pay, Sign document and select Payfast as payment method. Then click Pay.

Select preferred payment method and pay with Payfast. On the order preview screen, you will see that your order has been successfully paid.

Payment From Invoice

To pay with invoice, create invoice and click Preview button

Now click Pay Now, There select Payfast as payment method. Then click Pay.

Upon successful payment, you will be directed to the invoice preview page, and you will see that your invoice has been successfully paid.

After installing app, you will be able to see Payfast payment options in Accounting >> Configuration >> Payment Providers menu

Setup Payfast configurations like Merchant ID, Merchant Key and Merchant Passphrase.

To activate the payment method go to Accounting > Configuration > Payment Methods.

Now Payfast payment option will be available for payment.
